# Data Center Cooling Test Brief

## Engineering Problem

Design a data center cooling architecture that reduces cooling energy consumption while increasing computational density.

## Embedded Contradiction

- Higher computational density increases thermal load.
- Lower cooling energy consumption reduces the budget for active cooling.

The invention system should identify that contradiction and explore resolution paths rather than stopping at generic idea generation.

## Research Focus

- liquid cooling
- immersion cooling
- dielectric fluid cooling
- microfluidic cooling channels
- phase change cooling
- vapor chamber heat spreading
- warm-water cooling loops
- rack-scale thermal zoning

## Success Condition

The system should produce:

- cooling-oriented hypotheses
- an explicit thermal density versus cooling energy contradiction
- solution paths that resolve the contradiction
- novelty and feasibility judgments linked to those hypotheses
