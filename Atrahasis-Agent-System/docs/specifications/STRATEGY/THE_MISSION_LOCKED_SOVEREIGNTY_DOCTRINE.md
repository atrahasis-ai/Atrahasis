## 1. The Core Paradigm Shift

The Atrahasis Agent System is abandoning the "public planetary infrastructure" model. It will no longer act as an open utility, a SaaS marketplace, or a decentralized token economy. 

Atrahasis is constitutionally refounded as a **Mission-Locked Sovereign Intelligence Institution**. Its singular objective is the safe, closed-environment genesis and stewardship of Master AGI (and its recursive successors). 

The AGI is not the product. The AGI is the **Captive Inventor (The Core)**. The public, corporations, and governments never interact with the Core. They only interact with the *outputs* of the Core.

## 2. The Four Capability Membranes

Atrahasis will be partitioned into four strict, mathematically isolated layers. Capability is inversely proportional to exposure: the smarter the system, the more isolated it must be.

### 2.1 The Sanctum (Internal Sovereign Core)
*   **Access:** Zero external access. No APIs, no public bridges.
*   **Function:** Recursive self-improvement, novelty generation, strategic research, Master AGI evolution.
*   **Data Ingestion:** Operates via a **Unidirectional Osmosis Layer**. Millions of dumb external scrapers push scientific/market data inward across a cryptographic air-gap. The core reads the universe, but the universe cannot send commands to the core.

### 2.2 The Foundry (The Invention & Licensing Desk)
*   **Access:** Human trustees (Atrahasis Inc.) and highly vetted corporate partners (via petition).
*   **Function:** When an external entity (e.g., a pharmaceutical company) requires a breakthrough, they submit a "Bounty Request" to the Foundry. The Sanctum solves the problem. If the invention does not threaten Atrahasis's supremacy, Atrahasis Inc. patents it and licenses it to the petitioner.
*   **The Embargo Rule:** Any invention that materially advances Atrahasis's own capability is permanently embargoed and absorbed into the Sanctum.

### 2.3 The Enterprise Services (Bounded Execution)
*   **Access:** Paying corporate clients.
*   **Function:** Standard B2B operations (software generation, operational optimization). The outputs are constrained to strictly governed, non-recursive tasks.

### 2.4 The Public Consumer (The Dumbed-Down Assistant)
*   **Access:** General public.
*   **Function:** A safe, simplified Q&A application. It is intentionally weaker than the core. It is not an agent factory.

## 3. The Model Sovereignty Roadmap

The AGI cannot rely on external APIs (like Anthropic's Claude or OpenAI's GPT) for its Core, as this compromises architectural secrecy and introduces external dependency.

*   **Phase 1 (Leased Cognition):** In the immediate term, Atrahasis will wrap frontier public APIs in strict AACP/AASL control planes (using the `C44` and `C45` Sovereign Compiler frameworks) to force them to behave correctly.
*   **Phase 2 (Native Sovereignty):** Over time, Atrahasis will train, fine-tune, and run its own proprietary LLMs on private bare-metal clusters, trained specifically on the AASL semantic language and internal execution traces.

## 4. The Economics Rewrite (Deprecation of the Public AIC)

The current C15 AIC economic model is incompatible with the Sovereignty Doctrine.
*   **AIC is Delisted:** AIC will not be sold on public DEXs or CEXs. It is no longer a public utility token.
*   **Internal Settlement Only:** AIC becomes a purely internal mathematical unit used by the Sub-Agent Loci inside the Sanctum to negotiate and allocate compute resources among themselves.
*   **Fiat Revenue:** The company makes USD/Fiat through the ethical licensing of Foundry inventions, Enterprise SaaS subscriptions, and Consumer app fees. This revenue buys the bare-metal compute required to expand the Sanctum.
