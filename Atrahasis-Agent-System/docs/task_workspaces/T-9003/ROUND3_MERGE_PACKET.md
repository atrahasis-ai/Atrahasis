# Nanshe Round Three Merge Packet

No new inventions.

I think we should do it like this:

- Lock the doctrine under one name: `Mission-Locked Sovereignty Doctrine`.
- Atrahasis becomes a closed, mission-locked sovereign intelligence institution.
- The architecture is permanently four-surface:
  - `Sanctum`,
  - `Foundry`,
  - `Enterprise`,
  - `Public`.
- The constitutional operating principle is `closed capability, open accountability`.
- The AGI core is never sold and never exposed as a public or enterprise product surface.
- Revenue comes from bounded products, supervised services, petitioned invention outcomes, and selectively licensed derivative technologies.
- Recursion-critical novelty defaults to embargo.
- Outside data enters through one-way, quarantine-controlled intake.
- `AIC` should be internal-only settlement logic, not a public market-facing token.
- Model sovereignty is staged:
  - leased cognition now,
  - adapted private and open models next,
  - native Atrahasis models later.
- `C14`, `C15`, `C18`, and `C22` are the first mandatory rewrites.
- The next phase should be master redesign specification work, not more concept-level doctrine exploration.
