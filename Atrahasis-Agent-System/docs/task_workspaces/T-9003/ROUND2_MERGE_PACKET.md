# Nanshe Round Two Merge Packet

No new inventions.

I think we should do it like this:

- Atrahasis becomes a closed, mission-locked sovereign intelligence institution.
- The AGI itself is never the product and never becomes a public API.
- The architecture becomes four-surface:
  - `Sanctum` internal sovereign core,
  - `Foundry` petitioned invention and licensing,
  - `Enterprise` bounded business execution,
  - `Public` intentionally constrained assistant surface.
- The doctrine is `closed capability, open accountability`.
- Release law governs every output:
  - internal-only,
  - operator-only,
  - licenseable,
  - public-safe,
  - prohibited.
- Recursion-critical novelty defaults to embargo.
- Atrahasis sells bounded products, reviewed services, and selectively licensed derivative technologies, not raw AGI access.
- Outside data enters through one-way, quarantine-controlled intake.
- Frontier APIs may be used temporarily behind AACP/AASL control, but the long-term target is native model sovereignty.
- `C14`, `C15`, `C18`, and `C22` are the first mandatory rewrites.
