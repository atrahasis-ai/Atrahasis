## Round Two Swarm

### Lead Architect

1. `Sanctum`
   Internal-only sovereign core for recursive reasoning, invention, self-improvement planning, strategic research, AACP policy enforcement, and AASL-native orchestration.

2. `Foundry`
   Operator-mediated invention and licensing surface. External parties submit requests. Atrahasis decides whether the result is embargoed, retained, licensed, or rejected.

3. `Enterprise`
   Bounded business execution surface for software generation, forecasting, optimization, analysis, and controlled delivery. No unrestricted agent swarms, no raw novelty engine.

4. `Public`
   Safe assistant membrane with intentionally weaker capability and no recursion-critical access.

Implementation choice:
- move toward physical separation of `Sanctum`, but do not block the redesign on achieving perfect hardware isolation first;
- enforce logical isolation, policy isolation, audit isolation, and release isolation immediately.

- the AGI itself is never the product;
- the strongest inventions remain internal by default;
- the company monetizes bounded products, reviewed services, and selectively licensed derivative technologies;
- and any invention that materially strengthens Atrahasis's own recursive path is treated as internal strategic capital first.
