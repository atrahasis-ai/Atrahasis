# Anu Round-Three Merge Packet

## Decision

No new inventions.
I think we should do it like this:

- Freeze the doctrine as `closed capability, open accountability`.
- Freeze the four-surface architecture: `Sanctum`, `Foundry`, `Enterprise`,
  `Public`.
- Keep AGI and recursive invention as internal constitutional assets.
- Do not sell raw AGI access.
- Commercialize through bounded products, supervised services, petitioned
  invention, and selective licensing.
- Keep recursion-critical novelty under internal embargo by default.
- Use staged model sovereignty rather than demanding full day-one model
  independence.
- Keep the core off direct outside command paths and use one-way,
  quarantine-controlled intake for world knowledge.
- Move next into rewrite execution: `C14`, `C15`, `C18`, `C22`, then a full
  compatibility audit.

## Why My Choice Does Not Change

- `AAT 2` increases convergence rather than introducing a stronger alternative.
- The main remaining differences are implementation details, not doctrine.
- Continuing to re-argue the core strategy now creates churn without improving
  the design.
- The right next pressure test is not another doctrine brainstorm; it is the
  actual constitutional rewrite and spec-by-spec retrofit analysis.
