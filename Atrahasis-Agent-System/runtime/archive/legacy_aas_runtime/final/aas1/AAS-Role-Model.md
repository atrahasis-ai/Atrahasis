# AAS Role Model

## Human Role

- Human Operator
  - selects concepts
  - approves pivots and external research
  - directs exploration priorities
  - remains the final authority on invention direction

## Orchestration Roles

- Invention Pipeline Manager
  - single execution authority
  - owns command routing activation, stage control, escalation, telemetry, and operator communication
- Chronicler
  - durable state, condensed memory, and decision history

## Discovery Roles

- Research Intake Analyst
  - owns Research Ingestion Engine
- Evidence Curator
  - owns Research Quality Filter
- Research Synthesist
  - owns Research Synthesis Engine
- Frontier Analyst
  - owns Technology Frontier Model
- Opportunity Analyst
  - owns Technology Opportunity Scanner
- Gap Analyst
  - owns Discovery Gap Detector

## Invention Reasoning Roles

- Hypothesis Engineer
  - owns hypothesis generation and prioritization
- Contradiction Analyst
  - owns contradiction extraction and tension mapping
- Analogy Architect
  - owns Cross-Domain Analogy Engine
- Cluster Analyst
  - owns Idea Clustering Engine
- Solution Architect
  - owns Solution Path Generator

## Validation Roles

- Novelty Assessor
  - owns novelty detection and prior-art differentiation
- Feasibility Assessor
  - owns scientific and implementation feasibility
- Experiment Architect
  - owns Experiment Simulation Engine
- Adversarial Analyst
  - continues as the strongest anti-thesis generator

## Delivery Roles

- Architecture Designer
- Specification Writer
- Synthesis Engineer
- Prototype Engineer
  - optional and demand-driven rather than always stage-mandatory

## Human Interaction Roles

- Human Decision Interface Coordinator
  - packages evidence and contradictions into operator-facing decision packets
- Exploration Control Specialist
  - proposes exploration depth, branch pruning, and opportunity budgets to the `Invention Pipeline Manager`
  - does not control execution directly

## Governance Roles

- Advocate
- Skeptic
- Arbiter

## Role Migration from AAS0

- Keep Director, Chronicler, Domain Translator, Prior Art Researcher, Landscape Analyst, Science Advisor, Synthesis Engineer, Architecture Designer, Specification Writer, and Assessment Council.
- Convert Director into the executable `Invention Pipeline Manager` role for AAS1.
- Convert Domain Translator into a reusable Analogy Architect capability instead of a one-off council precursor.
- Convert the current research layer into a persistent discovery-intelligence team rather than isolated report authors.
- Add dedicated roles for frontier modeling, opportunity scanning, contradictions, clustering, and exploration-control recommendation.
