AGI Architecture Built on an AI-Agent Blockchain

Abstract

This document outlines a conceptual architecture for building Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) on top of a decentralized AInagent blockchain network.

Rather than relying on a single monolithic neural model, this design proposes a distributed intelligence composed of millions of specialized AI agents that coordinate through a blockchain-based consensus and memory system.

1. Introduction

Traditional AGI research focuses on scaling single models. However, biological intelligence and human civilization operate through distributed cognition.

The AI-Agent Blockchain can serve as a coordination substrate for large-scale machine intelligence where many agents collaborate economically and cognitively.
