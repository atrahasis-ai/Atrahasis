# Required Team and Resources for an AI-Agent Blockchain Scientific Discovery Project

Author: Joshua Dunn (Concept Originator)

Version: 1.0

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Human Expertise Required

Building a large-scale distributed AI system capable of assisting
scientific discovery requires collaboration across multiple disciplines.

### 1. Interdisciplinary Scientists

Researchers from multiple scientific domains are needed to guide the
system and interpret discoveries.

Examples include:

-   computational biologists\
-   molecular biologists\
-   chemists\
-   data scientists\
-   systems biologists

These scientists define research questions and validate results produced
by AI systems.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 2. Engineers

Engineers design and build the technical infrastructure that powers the
system.

Key engineering roles include:

-   machine learning engineers\
-   distributed systems engineers\
-   blockchain engineers\
-   data engineers\
-   infrastructure engineers

Their responsibility is to design scalable AI systems capable of
coordinating thousands or millions of AI agents.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### 3. Medical Researchers

Medical professionals ensure discoveries remain grounded in real
clinical science.

Examples include:

-   oncologists\
-   clinical researchers\
-   pharmacologists\
-   medical data analysts

Their role is to interpret findings, design experiments, and guide
clinical validation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Infrastructure Requirements

### 4. Large Computational Resources

Advanced AI research requires massive computing power.

Examples include:

-   GPU clusters\
-   high-performance computing centers\
-   cloud computing infrastructure\
-   large scientific datasets

These resources enable:

-   large-scale model training\
-   molecular simulations\
-   protein folding predictions\
-   drug interaction analysis

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Summary

Developing a distributed AI research system requires both human
expertise and powerful computational infrastructure.

Success depends on collaboration between:

-   interdisciplinary scientists
-   engineers
-   medical researchers
-   large-scale computing infrastructure

Together, these resources can enable powerful AI-assisted scientific
discovery platforms.
