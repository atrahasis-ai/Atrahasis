# Trinity Intelligence Mesh
## A Network Model for Multi-System Intelligence Collaboration

Author: Joshua Everett Dunn (Concept Originator)

---

# Overview

The **Trinity Intelligence Mesh** is a conceptual model describing how multiple trinities of systems
can interconnect to form a large intelligence network.

A **Trinity** consists of three cooperating components.

Examples include:

Human Intelligence
Artificial Intelligence
Blockchain Coordination

or

Artificial Intelligence
Blockchain
Verichain Verification

---

# Trinity Structure

A single trinity can be visualized as a triangle.

Each point represents a different intelligence component.

Edges represent interaction between those components.

---

# Mesh Expansion

When multiple trinities connect, they form a **mesh network**.

A mesh network means:

• every node can connect to multiple other nodes
• information flows across many pathways
• the network remains functional even if parts fail

---

# Mesh Characteristics

The Trinity Intelligence Mesh has the following properties:

• decentralized
• resilient
• scalable
• collaborative

Each trinity strengthens the overall network.

---

# Intelligence Flow

Information flows through the mesh using:

reasoning
verification
knowledge sharing

The mesh structure ensures ideas are:

checked
refined
expanded

before becoming accepted knowledge.

---

# Conceptual Role

The Trinity Intelligence Mesh acts as the **communication network**
for the Collective Intelligence system.
