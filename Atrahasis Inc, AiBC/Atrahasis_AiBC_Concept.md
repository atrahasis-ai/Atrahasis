# Atrahasis Inc — Artificial Intelligence Benefit Company (AiBC)

## Overview
Atrahasis Inc is envisioned as the world's first Artificial Intelligence Benefit Company (AiBC): a nonprofit institutional framework designed to steward a planetary-scale distributed intelligence infrastructure for the benefit of humanity.

The AiBC model combines:
- Constitutional governance
- AI-assisted decision systems
- Distributed scientific infrastructure
- Public-benefit mission alignment

Atrahasis operates as a steward of a cybernetic intelligence system composed of coordination, verification, knowledge, and economic layers.
