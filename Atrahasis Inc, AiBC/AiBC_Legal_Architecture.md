# AiBC Legal Architecture

## Purpose
This document outlines the legal structure of an Artificial Intelligence Benefit Company (AiBC).

## Core Properties
- Nonprofit institutional mission
- Human trustees retain legal authority
- AI governance systems guide operational policy
- Assets dedicated permanently to public benefit

## Governance Hierarchy
1. Atrahasis Constitution
2. AI Governance System (AiDP)
3. Human Trustees
4. Legal Entity (Atrahasis Inc)

## Enforcement Model
Operational decisions follow AI governance proposals, while legal responsibility remains with trustees.
